Technical Debt in AI-based Systems

An empirical study on the nature and evolution of technical debt in open-source AI projects.

Motivation & Details

AI-based systems accumulate a specific kind of technical debt that goes beyond code smells — data debt, model debt, configuration debt. We are mining large corpora of open-source ML repositories to characterise how this debt emerges, evolves, and gets repaid over time. We welcome contributions from researchers interested in MSR or software quality.

Why We Need You

Mining and classifying technical debt at scale requires a mix of static analysis skills, empirical software engineering methodology, and familiarity with ML codebases. We welcome researchers interested in repository mining (MSR), software quality, or AI/ML engineering who want to contribute to or collaborate on this study.
