Fairness-aware Software Engineering

A suite of tools to help practitioners detect and mitigate fairness issues in ML-intensive systems.

Motivation & Details

Fairness is not just an ethical concern — it is an engineering property that must be designed, measured, and maintained throughout the software lifecycle. We are developing tooling and methodologies to embed fairness checks into CI/CD pipelines and code review workflows. If you work on sociotechnical systems or bias detection, we would love to hear from you.

Why We Need You

Tackling algorithmic fairness requires input from diverse perspectives — sociotechnical researchers, practitioners who deal with real-world bias, and engineers who can translate fairness metrics into actionable tooling. If you work on bias detection, fairness auditing, or responsible AI, we want to hear from you.
