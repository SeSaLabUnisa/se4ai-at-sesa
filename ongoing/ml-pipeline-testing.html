Automated Testing for ML Pipelines

Novel techniques for automatically generating and executing test cases for machine learning pipelines.

Motivation & Details

Machine learning pipelines are notoriously hard to test: data dependencies, non-determinism, and the lack of traditional oracles make conventional testing tools inadequate. We are building automated approaches that target both data quality and model behaviour, drawing from metamorphic testing, mutation analysis, and coverage criteria adapted to the ML context. We are looking for collaborators with expertise in test automation or ML systems.

Why We Need You

Testing ML pipelines requires both software engineering expertise and machine learning intuition — a rare combination. We are looking for collaborators who can help bridge these worlds: whether you come from test automation, data engineering, or applied ML, your perspective matters. PhD students, postdocs, and industry practitioners are all welcome.
